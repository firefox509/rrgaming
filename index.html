About Us

We're the gaming club on campus at UTSA, we're also a Tespa chapter (Tespa South region). Not only are we a campus organization but we were also one of the first eSports clubs in the nation to gain club sport status. We mainly cater to PC games, but we play some console games, and some board and card games as well. We do not have weekly meetings but rather weekly meet-ups and casual games nights for specific games.

Games we Play

Flagship Games: League of Legends, Smash, CS:GO, Overwatch.

Supported Games: Minecraft, Dota 2, Heartstone, Board Games/Card Games Night.

Weekly Games:

  • League: 1st Friday of Each Month.
  • Smash: Filthy Casuals, Friday at 6PM in the Tejas Lounge.
  • Overwatch: Subject to change, check our Facebook each week.
  • Board Game Night: Tuesday at 7:30PM in the Tejas Lounge.

Our flashship games are the games we have directors and event coordinators to run LAN tournaments and online casual nights. The games we support outside of our flagship games are games we can allocated some resources to because the community is big enough, but we don't have enough support to run full-fledged LANs or events.

If your game is not currently supported, please send us a message and we'll get the ball rolling.
